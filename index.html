A bit about me

My name is Matthew Lavine and I am an Honors Junior Information Technology Major at the New Jersey Institute of Technology located in Newark, New Jersey.

I am currently employed at AdvancedGroup.net as a Web Developer and Junior System Administrator. I am mainly interested in Unix and Linux Systems Administration. My administration skills include experience administering Windows Server Environments, Linux Server Environments, Virtual Computing Environments, and Thin Computing Environments.

On a personal front, I love jazz, computers, and aviation. I am a jazz musician and the president / cofounder of the NJIT Society of Musical Arts where I lead a jazz band and play alto saxophone and trumpet. In my free time I enjoy working on my personal servers and building desktop computers for friends and colleagues. I am also training for my private pilot's license.
